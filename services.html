general service

A general bike service includes a variety of checks and adjustments to ensure your bike is in good condition.

Brakes: Checking the fluid level and brake pads, and adjusting them if needed

Tires: Checking the tread condition and inflating them to the correct pressure

Spark plug: Cleaning and replacing the spark plug to improve fuel efficiency and engine power

Battery: Checking the fluid level and ensuring it's charged

Air filter: Cleaning the air filter regularly

Fork oil: Checking the fork oil after a certain number of kilometers

bike moficication

Bike modification is the process of making changes to a bike to improve its performance, appearance, or safety.

Appearance: You can change the color, add decals, or install visors

Performance: You can upgrade the engine, exhaust system, suspension, or tires

Safety: You can install anti-theft devices

Minor modifications: Adding decals, stickers, or changing the color

Balance: Consider the balance between aesthetics and performance

engine tuing

the process of modifying a motorcycle engine by adjusting its internal components or electronic control unit (ECU) to improve.

Improve performance: Tuning can increase a motorcycle's power output, fuel efficiency, and durability.

Ensure smooth running: Tuning can help a motorcycle run smoothly and efficiently

Optimize engine design: Tuning can involve revising the design decisions made when the engine was developed.

Adjust the exhaust system: This can be done manually or through the engine's computer.

Adjust the carburetor: This can be done manually or through the engine's computer.

Adjust the spark plugs: This can be done manually or through the engine's computer.
